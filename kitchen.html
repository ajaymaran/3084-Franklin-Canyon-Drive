Kitchen

Dimensions: 27' x 16'
Area: 432 sq ft.

Designed with both style and functionality in mind, this expansive 432-square-foot chef's kitchen showcases a clean, contemporary aesthetic with warm natural wood cabinetry, sleek quartz countertops, and abundant built-in storage. A large center island with integrated sink and seating serves as the heart of the space, providing ample room for meal preparation, casual dining, and entertaining.

Premium stainless-steel appliances, including double wall ovens, are complemented by generous counter space and a thoughtfully designed layout that maximizes efficiency. Natural light fills the room through strategically placed windows and overhead skylights, creating a bright and inviting atmosphere throughout the day.

Blending modern elegance with everyday practicality, this beautifully appointed kitchen offers a seamless setting for everything from intimate family meals to large-scale entertaining.
