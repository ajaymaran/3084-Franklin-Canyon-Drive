Designed with matching luxury hospitality standards, the second bedroom serves as an inviting sanctuary. Daybed Suite & Office (Bedroom 3)

A highly adaptable architectural space configured as a cozy retreat or professional home studio. High-end built-in bookshelves with bronze and brass details provide excellent workspace storage. Features a luxury custom layout with a daybed nook overlooking the structural rock garden.

  • Ceiling Height 10.5 ft
  • Level Lower Wing (West)

Chef's Culinary Studio

An epicurean dream combining design precision with high-performance culinary machinery. Centered by a massive custom-carved Calacatta marble waterfall island, the kitchen includes Gaggenau double-ovens, professional induction burners, integrated Sub-Zero refrigeration, and hand-milled stained oak cabinetry.

  • Appliances Gaggenau & Sub-Zero
  • Level Main Level

Heated Horizon Infinity Pool

The ultimate outdoor centerpiece of the estate. Tucked into the cliffside terrace, the heated infinity-edge swimming pool cascades toward the breathtaking valley floor below. Flanked by an elegant Brazilian Ipe hardwood sun deck, comfortable chaise loungers, and an integrated fireside lounge table.

  • Dimensions 55 ft x 18 ft
  • Decking Premium Brazilian Ipe Decking
  • Heating Solar & Gas Dual System
  • Level Terrace Deck

Double-Height Living Salon

A magnificent open salon built to gather and captivate. High-volume, double-height ceilings combine with massive structural concrete columns and an elegant floating bronze fireplace. Triple-pane glass sliders retract completely to merge interior comforts with the main pool terrace deck.

  • Ceiling Height 22 ft double-height
